OUR MISSION

Our mission at Tech Duck is to bring a quality and completed job-seeking experience to you. Wherever you are on the tech-job seeking spectrum we want to assist you to success. Our platform is designed to be a resource to help all that are transitioning through the employment sector.

Have you ever stopped on a website to get what you needed done only to find out that, "Aww man, this doesn't have all that I need?" You then have to seek out another site hoping that you'll find what you need. We want Tech Duck to be your one stop shop. Our goal is to provide you with all the tools you need to carve out your personal career path. On this journey we strive to provide you with all the latest resources and trends to ensure employment success. The great thing here at Tech Duck is we cater to all fututre tech employees.
Do you have any suggestions on how we can be better for you? We embrace the challenge to get and be better. If there's a feature or enhancement you would like to see on our site; leave us a note at the bottom. We appreciate and welcome any and all feedback. Our mission and goal is to provide your experience at Tech Duck as one of simplicity, accomplishment and success.
-Tech Duck

Welcome.
Now that you have made that next step in creating 'Your' standout resume. Let's get started! We will begin with your general information and complete your resume with personal acheivements. When done, don't forget to hit SUBMIT to format your personal resume.

Tip:
Want to stick out to employers? When your resume includes a personal summary, you're twice as likely to hear back from the hiring manager. Value this space to summarize your progression in a professional capacity and highlight any notable skills and acheivements.
